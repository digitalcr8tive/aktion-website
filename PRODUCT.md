# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Brands, filmmakers, artists, agencies, and collaborators looking for a production partner for scripted, unscripted, and marketing work. Visitors need to understand AKtion!'s values, see the kind of work it produces, and quickly schedule a consultation.

## Product Purpose

Present AKtion! as a Black-owned, female-founded production company with a clear point of view about people, representation, and the culture of production. Success means visitors trust the team, understand the services, and either explore the portfolio or book a consultation.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, purposeful, and human. The voice is confident without being distant, values-led without becoming abstract, and creative without becoming chaotic. The central promise is: Imagine. Create. Inspire.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic agency templates, beige editorial minimalism, entertainment-industry visual clutter, repeated stock imagery, excessive gold-luxury styling, and pages that feel like duplicates with different headings.

## Design Principles

1. Let the work and the people share the frame.
2. Make both primary visitor paths obvious: view the work or book a consultation.
3. Give every page its own visual composition while keeping one unmistakable AKtion! voice.
4. Explain values through specific practices and language, not slogans alone.
5. Keep the experience direct, fast, and easy to navigate on any screen.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain strong contrast, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, descriptive alternative text, keyboard-operable navigation and booking controls, and reduced-motion support.
