Meet the team

Two founders. One shared conviction.

Meet the producers behind AKtion!’s people-first approach to scripted and unscripted storytelling.

The visionaries behind AKtion!

Ah’Sune McPhearson

Co-Founder and Head of Scripted Development & Production

Meet Ah’Sune McPhearson, Co-Founder and Head of Scripted Development & Production. She brings extensive on-set experience across television and film to every project she leads.

From Illinois to Atlanta, Ah’Sune has contributed her expertise to major productions including BET’s Boomerang Season 2; the upcoming feature Flowervale Street, starring Anne Hathaway; Apple TV+’s Me; Do Revenge; and The First Lady, starring Viola Davis. She holds a business degree from West Georgia Technical College and a bachelor’s degree from Georgia State University.

Known for her leadership, organization, and collaborative approach, Ah’Sune has built and led teams across countless independent productions. She has earned a reputation as a trusted professional who turns creative visions into reality efficiently, effectively, and without missing a beat.

Kyrah Kimbro

Co-Founder and Head of Unscripted Development & Production

Meet Kyrah Kimbro, Co-Founder and Head of Unscripted Development & Production. A Detroit native now based in Atlanta, Kyrah has brought her talents to some of the biggest networks in the industry, including A&E, Lifetime, BET, and TV One.

Her production credits range from A&E’s hit true-crime series The First 48 to TV One’s long-running docuseries For My Man. With several years in the industry, she has proven time and again that she can bring compelling stories to life across every format, from documentary and true crime to reality and beyond, including biopics.

Backed by a B.A. in Film from Michigan State University and an MBA from Georgia State University, Kyrah combines the instincts of a seasoned producer with the strategic mind of a business executive on every project she touches.
